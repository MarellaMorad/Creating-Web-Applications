Big Data Analyst Position Description

Big Data Analyst

  • Position Description Reference Number:

    TW123.

  • Position Title:

    Big Data Analyst.

  • Brief Description:

    TechWave is seeking a skilled Big Data Analyst to join our team. As a Big Data Analyst, you will be responsible for analyzing complex data sets to provide insights and develop data solutions for our clients.

  • Salary Range:

    Competitive salary based on experience.

  • Reports To:

    Data Analytics Manager.

  • Key Responsibilities:1

    • Collect, analyze and report data to meet business requirements
    • Import, clean, convert and analyze data to find insights
    • Present data through various visualizations
    • Design and develop relational databases for data collection

  • Essential Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes:1

    • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
    • 3+ years of experience in data analysis.
    • Experience with Big Data technologies such as Hadoop and Spark.
    • Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau and Power BI.
    • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
    • Excellent communication and interpersonal and teamwork skills.

  • Preferable Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes:1

    • Experience with Data Warehouse technologies such as Oracle, Informatica, and Teradata.
    • Experience with cloud-based Big Data platforms such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.
    • Experience with statistical modeling and machine learning algorithms.
