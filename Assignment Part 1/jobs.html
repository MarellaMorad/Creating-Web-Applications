Big Data Analyst

Position Description Reference Number: TW123

Position Title: Big Data Analyst.

Brief Description: TechWave is seeking a skilled Big Data Analyst to join our team. As a Big Data Analyst, you will be responsible for analyzing complex data sets to provide insights and develop data solutions for our clients.

Salary Range: Competitive salary based on experience.

Reports To: Data Analytics Manager.

Key Responsibilities:1

  • Collecting, analyzing, and reporting data for meeting several business requirements.
  • Importing/Collecting, cleaning, converting and analyzing the data for the purpose of find insights and making conclusions.
  • Presenting data in graphs, charts, tables, etc and designing and developing relational databases for collecting data.
Essential Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes:1
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in data analysis.
  • Experience with Big Data technologies such as Hadoop, Spark, and Hive.
  • Experience with programming languages such as Python, Java, and R.
  • Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, QlikView, and Power BI.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment.
Preferable Qualifications, Skills, Knowledge, and Attributes:1
  • Master's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Experience with Data Warehouse technologies such as Oracle, Informatica, and Teradata.
  • Experience with cloud-based Big Data platforms such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.
  • Experience with statistical modeling and machine learning algorithms.
  • Experience with NoSQL databases such as MongoDB, Cassandra, and HBase.
A typical day in the life of a Big Data Analyst at TechWave might look something like this:
  1. Start the day with a quick stand-up meeting with the team to discuss progress and challenges.
  2. Review and analyze large volumes of data from various sources using analytical tools and programming languages to extract, manipulate, and clean data.
  3. Develop and maintain data models, create data pipelines, and build data warehouses using analytical tools and programming languages.
  4. Run statistical analyses to identify trends and insights that can help clients make better business decisions.
  5. Create data visualizations using tools like Tableau or PowerBI to share insights with stakeholders.
  6. Take a break during the day and enjoy the well-stocked and equipped kitchen with a view of Melbourne City.
Overall, being a Big Data Analyst at TechWave is a challenging and exciting experience that involves working on important projects with a supportive and collaborative team. It's all about using data to help clients make better decisions, and the day-to-day work involves a mix of coding, analysis, and creativity.
